About
Elegant Cornicing

A leading manufacturer and distributor of polystyrene cornices and hardware products across the Southern African market since 2011.

Our Journey

From One Client
to Thousands of Stores

Elegant Cornicing opened its doors for trading in 2011, winning over its very first customers in the retail trade industry — including its first major National Chain Store. The business has grown exponentially since then and our product offering can now be found in thousands of retail stores across Southern Africa.

The key reason for our success has been the ability to consistently supply high quality products at affordable prices. As the business continues to grow, we expanded our manufacturing and warehousing facility to over 3000㎡ in 2016, with 9 branches and distributors throughout Southern Africa.

The company is extremely passionate about offering affordable and elegant products, allowing the consumer to truly transform their house into a home. Through our ongoing innovative nature, we always try to be first to market with new ideas and designs — hence there are always patents pending so we can offer our clients new and exciting products.

Additional products imported from Europe and China make up the balance of the Elegant Range.

Our Core Values

Quality First
Every cornice we produce meets exacting standards. Our cutting-edge automated facility ensures consistency in every batch, every time.
Affordable Excellence
We believe premium finishing shouldn't cost a premium. Local manufacturing keeps our prices competitive without compromising quality.
Continuous Innovation
Our R&D programme and engineering team are always developing new designs, with patents pending on our latest innovations.
Green Manufacturing
We're proud to operate an in-house EPS recycling machine, turning manufacturing waste into reusable pellets and reducing landfill impact.
Proudly South African
Manufactured locally, delivered locally. Our own vehicle fleet ensures orders reach our clients reliably across Southern Africa.
Retail Partnership
Best-in-industry POS material and packaging supports our retail partners — driving sales through the till with minimal stock write-offs.
